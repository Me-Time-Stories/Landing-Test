Our Stories

Designed for all occasions and customisable for all backgrounds, our stories are entertaining, educational, and empowering. They allow children to embrace what makes them them (and have a lot of fun along the way!).

The Power of MeTime Personalisation

Research shows that personalisation in children's storybooks aids in literacy development, which in turn helps kids grow into confident and eager readers.

Living out their wildest dreams on paper and relating to their personalised avatars makes children more receptive to the critical teachings of each tale they read — whether it be bravery, companionship, kindness, or curiosity.  

MeTime Stories offers a level of storybook personalisation unlike any other. We don't stop at eye colour and hairstyle. Children use our story customisation tool to bring their avatars to life, letting their imaginations run wild across a series of stories.

Our Goal

It's our mission to ignite a passion for reading among today's youth with personalised children's stories. We want to enable your kids to explore fictional worlds with personal avatars. From reliving past experiences to exploring different cultures to saving the day as a superhero, we aim to engage child readers like never before.
